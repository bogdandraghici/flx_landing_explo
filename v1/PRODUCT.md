# PRODUCT.md — FlowX landing (v1 exploration)

## Product purpose

Marketing site for **FlowX.AI**: enterprise AI agents for regulated industries
(banking, insurance, logistics). The pitch: 95% of AI initiatives fail; FlowX
puts you in the 5% — 220+ production-proven agents, deployable in weeks, wired
into legacy systems. The site must read as *infrastructure-grade*: calm,
precise, technical, expensive.

## Register

`brand` — this is a marketing/landing property; design IS the product. But the
brand voice is engineering restraint, not campaign loudness: closer to
Linear / Palantir than to a SaaS splash page.

## Users

- Enterprise buyers (COO / CIO / heads of ops at banks, insurers, logistics).
  Skeptical, burned by AI pilots. They need proof and control, not hype.
- Senior engineers/architects evaluating feasibility — they notice terminal
  motifs, mono type, real diagrams, and they smell fake polish instantly.

## Tone & voice

- Confident, terse, evidence-first. Numbers and section indices (`01 /`,
  `002 —`) as a mono "annotation layer" over display copy.
- Terminal/compiler metaphors are load-bearing (the hero is a "solution
  compiler"). Data voice = Geist Mono, uppercase, tracked-out labels.

## Strategic principles

- **One accent.** Brand amber `#FCB813` only, used sparingly — resolution,
  emphasis, current-state. Never decorative washes of it.
- **Hairline architecture.** Structure from 1px lines and subtle ink veils,
  not from card boxes. Depth in dark = painted highlights; in light = soft
  real shadow.
- **Everything themed.** Dark default + neutral-paper light theme share one
  token contract; nothing hardcodes a white/charcoal.
- **Motion honors `prefers-reduced-motion`**; reduced = resolved pose, crisp
  snaps over mush.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS gradient-mesh heroes, glassmorphism, multi-color accent salads.
- Bootstrap-y boxed navbars, chunky drop shadows, bouncy easing.
- Anything that looks like a template. This site's value is bespoke restraint.
