Platform · Overview

One platform to put AI to work — on the systems you already run.

Most vendors hand you a tool and leave the hard parts to you. FlowX.AI is the whole environment — design, run, integrate and govern — so a regulated customer or operations journey goes from idea to production in weeks, without replacing your core and without a pile of custom glue code.

A “platform” isn't one feature. It's everything a journey needs — the canvas, the engine, the connections, the experience, the intelligence and the controls — in one place.

That's the difference between a demo that works once and software that runs a bank's lending, a insurer's claims, or a carrier's operations every day. The AI model sits inside as one bounded, observed step — so what you ship is reliable and explainable, not a black box you hope behaves.

01 / Building blocks

What's inside the platform.

Six parts that work as one. You don't have to adopt them all at once — most teams start with one journey and grow.

Design

Design studio

Draw journeys, screens, data and integrations on one visual canvas — business and engineering together, without piles of glue code.

Run

Journey engine

Executes your business processes and coordinates people, systems and data in real time, so a journey behaves the same way every time it runs.

Connect

Integration layer

Plugs into the systems you already run — mainframes, Temenos, FIS, databases, cloud services — through ready-made connectors and APIs. No rip-and-replace.

Experience

Web & mobile apps

Renders responsive web and mobile experiences from the same definitions, updated live — one build, every channel.

Intelligence

AI & agents

Build and run AI agents that decide and assist — placed as bounded, observable steps inside the flow, never an unaccountable black box.

Trust

Security & governance

Role-based access, workspace isolation for large organizations, and a full audit trail across everything — built for regulated work.

02 / In the enterprise

Why it holds up under real scrutiny.

Wraps, not replaces

Modernize on top of legacy cores instead of a multi-year rip-and-replace.

Enterprise scale

Event-driven and Kubernetes-native — thousands of concurrent journeys.

Multi-tenant

Complete workspace isolation for large, multi-brand organizations.

Auditable by design

OAuth2, fine-grained permissions and comprehensive audit trails throughout.
