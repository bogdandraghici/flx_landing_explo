What cookies are

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They let the site remember your actions and preferences over time, and help the operator understand how the site is used. FlowX.AI uses cookies and similar technologies on flowx.ai and related properties.

Categories of cookies we use

Cookies on our site fall into the standard categories below. You control non-essential categories through the consent banner shown on your first visit.

  • Necessary — required for the site to function (security, load balancing, remembering your consent choices). These cannot be switched off.
  • Preferences — remember choices you make (such as language or region) to personalize your experience.
  • Statistics — help us understand how visitors use the site, in aggregate, so we can improve it.
  • Marketing — used to measure and improve the relevance of our campaigns; may be set by us or third parties.

Managing your preferences

You can accept or reject non-essential cookies, and change your choice at any time, using the cookie settings on our site. You can also block or delete cookies through your browser settings, though some parts of the site may not work as intended if necessary cookies are disabled.
